# TabPet Roadmap

## Product Direction

TabPet is intended to feel less like a floating widget and more like a tiny creature inhabiting the browser viewport.

## Core Experience Goals

- roam across the whole screen instead of staying in a fixed corner
- support drag-and-drop repositioning
- expose visible needs such as hunger, energy, and joy
- allow fast interactions such as feeding, sleeping, and play
- support user-supplied visuals later without rewriting the behavior layer

## Near-term Milestones

1. Full-screen roaming pet
2. Drag and placement interactions
3. Replaceable art assets
4. Better animations and effects
5. Saved long-term pet progression
